<h2>Three-Time World Champion</h2><p>NAC — Nueva Alcantara Club (Av. de Barcelona, 8, San Pedro de Alcántara) has been named the World's Best Padel Club by PadelSpain three consecutive years: 2023, 2024, and 2025. The award judges investment, management, infrastructure quality, services, innovation, and user experience — and NAC has topped the list every single time. Over 400,000 visitors pass through its gates every year, making it one of the busiest padel destinations in Europe.</p><h2>The Courts: 16 Panoramic Outdoor Courts</h2><p>All 16 courts at NAC are outdoor and panoramic, surfaced with premium artificial grass. The centrepiece is the Range Rover Central Court — a super-panoramic stadium court with grandstands that hosts A1 Padel Tour events, FIP Platinum tournaments, and the annual PadelSpain World Padel Awards ceremony. The remaining 15 panoramic courts are floodlit until 23:00, making evening sessions a genuine pleasure in the warm Marbella air.</p><ul><li>16 outdoor panoramic courts on artificial grass</li><li>Range Rover Central Court — stadium format with grandstands</li><li>Floodlit until 23:00 daily</li><li>Open every day 08:00–23:00</li><li>Book via Taykus: nac.taykus.com</li></ul><h2>The M3 Padel Academy</h2><p>The on-site M3 Padel Academy is widely regarded as one of Europe's top coaching institutions. A bilingual team of six in-house coaches delivers everything from beginner group clinics and junior development programmes to high-performance training, coach education courses, and international padel tour packages. In 2024 alone the academy hosted over 3,000 sports tourists through organised padel tours.</p>

<h2>Beyond the Courts: THE I/O Gym, Soul by Breath & Oxygen SPA</h2><p>NAC is far more than a padel club. THE I/O Concept gym covers 2,000 m² of indoor and outdoor space and offers HIIT, yoga, pilates, power classes, and personal training. The Soul by Breath restaurant serves Mediterranean cuisine all day from a sun-drenched terrace overlooking the courts. For recovery, the Oxygen SPA features a normobaric oxygen chamber (30 m²) and professional physiotherapy. An official Adidas pro shop and the Oculto Hair Club complete the lifestyle offering.</p>

<h2>Membership & Booking — No Obligation Required</h2><p>NAC is an open club — no membership is needed to play. Simply book a court via the Taykus platform at nac.taykus.com or contact the reception via WhatsApp (+34 639 779 315). For regular players, three membership tiers are available: Paddle Member (€60/month), Morning Member (€120/month), and the all-inclusive Black Member (€450/month) which includes unlimited court access, gym entry, a locker, and two central court matches per month. Court hire starts at around €24/hour for standard courts.</p>
